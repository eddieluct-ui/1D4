About Penang

Penang is one of Malaysia’s most vibrant destinations, known for its rich history, multicultural society, and beautiful coastal scenery. George Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with heritage buildings and modern creativity.

Famous Food

Char Kway Teow

Smoky stir-fried noodles with prawns and rich flavour.

Asam Laksa

Spicy, tangy fish noodle soup unique to Penang.

Nasi Kandar

Rice served with flavourful curries and side dishes.

People & Culture

Penang is a melting pot of cultures including Malay, Chinese, and Indian communities. This diversity is reflected in its festivals, architecture, and everyday lifestyle, creating a unique and vibrant cultural experience.

Famous Places

George Town

Historic streets with murals and heritage buildings.

Penang Hill

Scenic hilltop views and cool refreshing air.

Kek Lok Si Temple

One of Southeast Asia’s most iconic temples.
